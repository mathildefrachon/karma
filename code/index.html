Who are we ?

Karma is a non-profit organisation that helps businesses having an online visibility and improve their sustainable approach by providing a visual customer feedback.

Therefore we designed a guide to localize restaurants, cafés, shops etc... in Copenhagen with the desire of letting you speak out your own concerns.

Why doing this ?

The consciousness of the impact of our consumption on our planet is rising and people have more and more the need to change their habits to more sustainability and respect of Nature.

By living in a big city like Copenhagen, it is not always easy to stop our busy life to look for where to shop our groceries for a zero waste consumption, where to provide ethical products or where to eat seasonal, local or vegetarien.

Meanwhile, a lot of new ideas, start-ups, small businesses are emerging with a strong will to make a change in the business world by being concern of their ecological impact from the beginning and try to minimise it for more sustainability.

HOW DOES IT WORK ?

Karma allows you to tell your experience directly to the businesses.

You are looking for a restaurant that provides vegetarian dishes. Thanks to our filters, you find a place that fulfil your needs.

After a good dinner, you were very pleased with the vegetarian options of the menu but you had a very big portion and the waiter didnt have the possibility to make a take way.

Disappointed by the waste of food, you scan Karma’s QR code on the venue and are redirected to the rating page of Karma’s webpage.

You choose to rate on ‘vegetarian friendly’ saying you approve and had a great experience and decide to express your concern by rating ‘zero waste’ as well and leave a comment to the business.

On his Karma application, the restaurant will receive your grade and its average for ‘zero waste’ will go down. With your comment, he will appreciate your feedback and understand that he has to improve in this area.
